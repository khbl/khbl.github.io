I'm a Research Fellow at the Duke Clinical Research Institute at Duke University.

My main research interests are the application of machine learning, mathematical optimization, and data analytics methods to resuscitation, emergency medical services, and pre-hospital response to time-urgent medical conditions, such as cardiac arrest, drug overdose, and stroke.
