I'm a PhD candidate in the Department of Mechanical and Industrial Engineering at the University of Toronto under the supervision of Professor Timothy C.Y. Chan. I'm also an honourary clinical data analyst at the Scottish Ambulance Service. My primary research interests are the application of optimization and analytics methods in resuscitation science and pre-hospital medicine.

I previously completed my Master of Applied Science and Bachelor of Applied Science degrees at the University of Toronto.
