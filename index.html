I'm a PhD candidate in the Department of Mechanical and Industrial Engineering at the University of Toronto under the supervision of Professor Timothy C.Y. Chan. I'm also an honourary clinical data analyst at the Scottish Ambulance Service.

My primary research interests are the application of optimization and data analytics methods to resuscitation, emergency medical services, and pre-hospital response to time-urgent medical conditions.

I previously completed my Master of Applied Science (2019) and Bachelor of Applied Science (2017) at the University of Toronto. In 2022, I was a visiting postgraduate student at the University of Edinburgh.
